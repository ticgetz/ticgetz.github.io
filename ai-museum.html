01 / Our Vision

Not a museum.
A movement.

A revolutionary fusion of Artificial Intelligence, science and human potential, designed to inspire, educate and empower future generations.

India's next century will be defined not by what it consumes, but by what it creates. The One Earth, One Heart AI Museum is built to make that future tangible: a place where students become scientists, visitors become believers, and a country sees its own ambition reflected back in glass, steel and code.

This is not just a building. It is a symbol of Future India. A place where science meets imagination, and dreams become reality.

03 / What Makes It Special

Six ideas, built into every square metre.

Every element carries one conviction: India can build a public space the world will travel to see.

P / 01

Brain-inspired architecture

A 5-acre campus shaped like a human brain. A symbol of intelligence, creativity and limitless imagination, rendered as permanent public art.

P / 02

Galaxy observation tower

India's largest public telescope, a transparent sky-elevator, and an observation deck for the universe.

P / 03

AI and innovation experiences

Hands-on labs, immersive demos and live AI installations. Students don't read about technology, they shape it.

P / 04

Underwater entry experience

A submarine-inspired tunnel that opens the journey. An emotional entry into another world.

P / 05

Sustainable and green campus

Solar power, rainwater harvesting, recycling and smart infrastructure across every acre.

P / 06

Designed for everyone

Full accessibility, multilingual AI assistance, family-friendly facilities and world-class safety systems.
